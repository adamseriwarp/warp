## ‚ùì Common Questions

**Q: What if I can't find a carrier?**  
A: Run `python get_carrier_list.py` to see all 604+ carriers, or check if the carrier has had 5+ shipments in the last 4 weeks.

**Q: Do I need to match the exact capitalization?**  
A: No! The workflow is case-insensitive. `ILLYRIAN TRANSPORT LLC` = `illyrian transport llc`

**Q: What about special characters like & or '?**  
A: Include them exactly as shown. For example: `C & J'S METRO TRUCKING LLC`
